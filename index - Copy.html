Weekly Meetings

The club meets every Friday from 1pm to 4 pm. In Main hall room 303. However the room is open at 10 am, so all are welcome to come in between then. We also offer help on homework and labs to students struggling.

Hackathons

Hackathons are a great way to get out, meet people, make professional connections, as well as make friends and have fun. They are also a great way for you to branch out and learn outside of the class room. They are completely free. They range from 24 to 48 hour hackathons, and are always alot of fun.

You do not need to know how to code to attend.

Club Resources

We currently have the following resources as a club at our disposal. Club members can use these at any time during the meeting, please just inform one of our officers and we will get it from storage. The list is as follows:

  • Raspberry Pi
  • Arduino
  • GameMaker RPG software

HacKSU Meetings

The main campus computer club goes by the name HacKSU. We do attend their meetings that happen on Tuesday from 7:00 pm to 8:30 pm at the main campus. These meetings are usually very fun, and very educational. So bring your laptops. To further student access, we have formed a carpool to the main campus, every Tuesday. We leave at 5:15 pm from the Stark Main hall basement. Please contact a club officer if you are interested in going. We will wait as long as 5:30 if given prior notice. The carpool is completely free, and we only use Tylers, Sammi or my vehicle. Depending upon where you live, we may be able to drop you off at your house on the return trip from Main campus.
