About

The Kent State Stark Computer Club is a student organization at the Kent State University Stark campus focused on the promotion of education in the area of computer science and in technology in general as it relates to everyday life.

How can I involve myself?

Kent State Stark Computer Club meets every Friday from 1pm to 4pm in room 303 of the Main Hall. The room is open for anyone from 10am-3pm.

We also offer help on homework and labs to students struggling.

You can also like our Facebook page to find out more about us
